## 🚀 Quick Tips for Efficient Gameplay

1. **Master Your HUD** – Use a 4-finger claw setup (or at least 3-finger) to aim, move, and shoot simultaneously. Adjust button opacity/placement until it feels natural.
2. **Pre-aim & Crosshair Placement** – Always keep your crosshair at head/chest level where enemies are likely to appear. It reduces the time needed to snap onto targets.
3. **Slide Cancelling** – Chain slide → jump to break cameras and move faster around corners. Practice in training mode.
4. **Sound is Everything** – Use headphones and pay attention to footsteps. The "Alert" perk can be a crutch until your ears are trained.
5. **Map Knowledge > Gun Skill** – Learn common power positions, spawn points, and rotation paths. This alone will win you more games.
6. **Don't Over-extend** – Kills come naturally when you hold smart angles. Play your life in Search & Destroy and Hardpoint.
7. **Stick to a Few Guns** – Master 2-3 weapons from different classes (AR, SMG, Sniper) before hopping onto the meta train.

## 🐺 Camo Grinding – The Essentials

Grinding completionist camos (Damascus!) is a marathon, not a sprint. Here's my approach:

- **Play Hardpoint Shipment** – The tightest map, most engagements per minute. Perfect for crouch, hipfire, headshot, and no-attachment kills.
- **Stack Challenges** – For example, go for *no attachments*, *hipfire*, and *full-attachment* kills in the same game mode to save time.
- **Launchers & Melee First** – The hardest weapons to grind. Work on them passively while grinding primaries.
- **Use Double Weapon XP Cards** – Always use them when leveling up a fresh gun to unlock crouch/headshot challenges earlier.
- **Diamond Camo** – After unlocking Gold, the grind for Diamond (10 kills per match × 150 matches) is best done in Free-For-All or Hardpoint Shipment.

For the full step-by-step guide, check out [`camos/complete-camo-guide.md`](camos/complete-camo-guide.md) *(guide coming soon – stay tuned!)*
